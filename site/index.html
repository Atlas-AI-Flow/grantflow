About GrantFlow

Finding funding shouldn't be a full-time job. GrantFlow is an open directory designed to help allied health professionals and families discover financial support without sifting through endless Google results.

We aggregate data from public foundations and verified sources daily. We are not a grant-maker; we simply connect you to the application sources.
