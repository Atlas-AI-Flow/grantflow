About This Opportunity

ACT Today! provides quarterly grants up to $5,000 to help families access autism-related therapies including speech, OT, and ABA that they otherwise could not afford.

Eligibility Requirements

Families of children with autism. Priority given to households with income under $100,000. Funding covers ABA, speech therapy, OT, medications, and home safety devices.
