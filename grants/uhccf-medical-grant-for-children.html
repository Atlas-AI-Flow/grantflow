About This Opportunity

UHCCF provides grants to help children access medical services including speech therapy, occupational therapy, and physical therapy not fully covered by insurance.

Eligibility Requirements

Families with children who need health-related services not covered or not fully covered by commercial health insurance. Up to $5,000/year per child ($10,000 lifetime max). Do NOT need UnitedHealthcare insurance.
